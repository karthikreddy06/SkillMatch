## 1. App Descriptions

### Short Description (Max 80 chars)

### Full Description (Max 4000 chars)
**SkillMatch** is the ultimate platform connecting job seekers with employers through intelligent, skills-based matching.

**For Job Seekers:**
*   **AI Recommendations:** Get job suggestions tailored to your skills and profile.
*   **Easy Apply:** Create your profile once and apply to multiple jobs with a single tap.
*   **Track Applications:** Stay updated on your application status in real-time.
*   **Map View:** Find jobs near you with our interactive map integration.

**For Employers:**
*   **Post Jobs Quickly:** Detailed job postings with customizable requirements.
*   **Smart Filtering:** View candidates who match your exact criteria.
*   **Direct Chat:** Communicate directly with applicants within the app.

**Key Features:**
*   **Secure & Private:** Your data is protected.
*   **User-Friendly Interface:** Intuitive design for seamless navigation.
*   **Real-time Notifications:** Never miss an opportunity or message.

Download SkillMatch today and take the next step in your career journey!
