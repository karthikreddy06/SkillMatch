Request Account Deletion

Warning: Deleting your account is permanent. All your data, including profile, resumes, and application history, will be permanently removed.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the SkillMatch App associated with your account.
  2. Navigate to Settings > Privacy & Security.
  3. Tap on Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your choice.

Alternatively, you can contact our support team at support@simats.edu.in with the subject line "Delete Data Request". Please include your registered email address.
