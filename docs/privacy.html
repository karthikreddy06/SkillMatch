Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

SkillMatch ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, update your profile, or communicate with us.

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, password, and role (Job Seeker or Employer).
  • Profile Information: Resume data, skills, education, experience, and profile picture.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including:

  • Matching job seekers with employers.
  • Processing applications.
  • Sending notifications about job matches or messages.

3. Data Deletion

You can request to delete your account and associated data at any time. Visit our Delete Account page for instructions.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us at support@simats.edu.in.
